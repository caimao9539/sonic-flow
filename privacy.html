Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 19, 2026

At Sonic Flow, we value the privacy of our visitors and customers. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, store, and utilize your personal information when using our API gateway services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect essential data to maintain secure routing infrastructure:

  • Account Details: Name, developer email address, and company name provided during registration.
  • Operational Metadata: Incoming client IP addresses, route request paths, latency logs, and rate-limit statistics to troubleshoot gateway errors.
  • Cookies: Standard session tokens used to securely identify logged-in developers.

2. Payment Processing

All subscription processing is securely handled by our third-party merchant processor, Stripe. Sonic Flow does not store or process cardholder credit/debit card numbers on its systems. The transmission of billing details is fully encrypted and compliant with PCI-DSS guidelines.

3. How We Use Information

We process collected developer data for security and performance purposes:

  • To provision active API keys and custom domains.
  • To prevent abuse, script attacks, and quota violations.
  • To provide technical and invoice billing support.

4. Data Transfers and GDPR

We adopt GDPR standard safeguards for European residents. You hold rights to access, amend, or purge your account records. Inquiries can be forwarded directly to support@sonicapi.net.
